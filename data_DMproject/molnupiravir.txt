molnupiravir 200mg is an antiviral medicine that works by stopping the virus that causes COVID-19 from growing and spreading. It’s used to treat early COVID-19 infection and can help with severe symptoms. It is only available with prescription and as capsules. only adults ages 18 and above can use this treatment. some common side effects is dizziness, headaches, diarrhea, nausea and vomiting.  The recommended dosage is to take 4 capsules in the morning and 4 capsules in the evening. molnupiravir is not suitable for pregnant, trying to get pregnant or breastfeeding and if you have some allergic reaction to other medications.
