Antibiotic: Amoxicillin
• Suitable for men, women, and children of all 
ages.
Form:tablets
• Age group: Generally prescribed for individuals 
of all ages, including infants.
• Dosage: Typically ranges from 250mg to 
500mg, taken every 8 hours.
• Contraindications: Individuals with a history of 
severe allergic reactions to penicillin should 
avoid.
• Side effects: Common side effects include 
nausea, diarrhea, and rash.
• Prescription: Requires a prescription from a 
healthcare provider
