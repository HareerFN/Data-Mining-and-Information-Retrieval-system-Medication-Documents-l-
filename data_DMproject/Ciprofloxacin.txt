Antibiotic: Ciprofloxacin
• Purpose: Used to treat bacterial infections in 
adults.
Form:tablets
• Suitability: Suitable for men and women, not 
typically prescribed for children.
• Age Group: Generally prescribed for adults 
aged eighteen and above.
• Dosage: Varies based on the type and severity 
of infection, typically ranging from 250 to 750 
milligrams, taken twice daily.
• Contraindications: Not recommended for 
pregnant or breastfeeding women, as well as 
individuals with a history of tendon disorders.
• Side Effects: Common side effects include 
nausea, diarrhea, and headache.
• Prescription: Requires a prescription from a 
healthcare provider
