Profen 400mg is a painkiller used to treat various types of pain like headaches, migraines, toothaches, and arthritis. It is available in tablet form and does not require a prescription. Side effects of Profen may include rash, swelling, difficulty breathing, gastrointestinal bleeding, kidney problems, and severe skin reactions. The recommended dosage for adults and adolescents is 1-2 tablets, 3-4 times a day, not exceeding 1200 mg per day. For children aged 6-12 years, the usual dose is one 200 mg tablet, 1-3 times a day, with a maximum of 3 tablets per day.
