Panadol Extra is a painkiller used for various purposes such as muscular aches, period pain, arthritis/osteoarthritis, toothache, migraine headache, colds and flu, tension headache, sinus pain/headache, and backache. It is available in tablet form and does not require a prescription. Common side effects may include dizziness, headache, constipation, insomnia, restlessness, anxiety, and digestive upset. The recommended dosage for adults and children over 12 years is 1-2 tablets every 4-6 hours, with a maximum of 8 tablets in 24 hours. Panadol Extra should not be taken by individuals with a previous history of hypersensitivity to paracetamol, caffeine, or any of the excipients used in the product.
