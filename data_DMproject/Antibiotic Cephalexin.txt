Antibiotic: Cephalexin
• Purpose: Used to treat bacterial infections such as skin infections, respiratory tract infections, urinary tract infections, and bone infections.
• Form: Liquid suspension for oral administration.
• Suitability: Suitable for both adults and children.
• Age Group: Prescribed for individuals of all ages, including infants, children, and adults.
• Dosage: Dosage varies depending on the severity of the infection and the age of the patient. Typically, the dosage ranges from 25 to 100 milligrams per kilogram of body weight per day, divided into two to four doses.
• Contraindications: Avoided by individuals with a known allergy to cephalosporin antibiotics.
• Side Effects: Common side effects may include diarrhea, nausea, vomiting, and stomach pain.
• Prescription: Requires a prescription from a healthcare provider before use.
