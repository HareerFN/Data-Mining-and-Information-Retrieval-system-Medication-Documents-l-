
Roflumilast is a selective phosophodiesterase-4 inhibitor indicated to decrease the risk of exacerbation in patients with sever chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD). Roflumilast comes as a table of 250mg or 500mg to take my mouth. only used for 6 years old and above and only with prescription. some common side effects is diarrhea, nausea, dizziness, headache, back pain, muscle spasm, decrease in appetite and uncontrollable shaking of a part of your body. The recommended dosage is 500mg per day. you must advise from the doctor before using this medication if you have liver disease allergic to roflumilast, history of mental health conditions, pregnant, planning to get pregnant or breastfeeding.
